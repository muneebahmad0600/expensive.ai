What happens when your account is deleted

  • All expense data linked to your account (including text and voice-created entries) will be permanently deleted from our databases.
  • Any backup or synced data stored in cloud services (e.g., Firebase) will also be removed.
  • Some data (such as logs required for legal, security, or fraud-prevention purposes) may be retained for up to 30 days before permanent deletion.

Data that may be retained

We may keep the following limited data temporarily for legitimate reasons:

  • Error or crash logs (up to 30 days)
  • Aggregated, anonymized analytics data that cannot identify you

Verification

To protect your account, we may ask for identity verification (such as the email associated with your account) before deletion.
